Frequently Asked Questions

How do I sell my books?

+

Simply create an account, go to your seller dashboard, and click "Add New Book". Fill in the book details, upload photos, and set your price. Your book will be listed for other users to browse and purchase.

How do I buy books?

+

Browse our collection of books, add them to your cart, and proceed to checkout. You can pay securely through our integrated payment system and track your order status.

What payment methods do you accept?

+

We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, and digital wallets like PayPal. All transactions are processed securely through our payment partners.

How do I track my order?

+

Once your order is placed, you'll receive a confirmation email with tracking information. You can also check your order status in your buyer dashboard.
